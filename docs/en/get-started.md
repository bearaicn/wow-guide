# Beginner's Guide

Welcome to World of Warcraft! Whether you're a new adventurer stepping into Azeroth for the first time or a returning veteran, this guide will help you get started quickly.

## Choose Your Faction

Before beginning your adventure, you need to choose a faction:

### Alliance
- **Races**: Human, Dwarf, Night Elf, Gnome, Draenei, Worgen, Void Elf, Lightforged Draenei, Dark Iron Dwarf, Kul Tiran, Mechagnome
- **Main Cities**: Stormwind, Ironforge, Darnassus
- **Characteristics**: Order, Honor, Justice

### Horde
- **Races**: Orc, Undead, Tauren, Troll, Blood Elf, Goblin, Pandaren, Nightborne, Highmountain Tauren, Mag'har Orc, Vulpera
- **Main Cities**: Orgrimmar, Thunder Bluff, Undercity
- **Characteristics**: Freedom, Unity, Strength

## Choose Your Class

World of Warcraft offers 12 classes to choose from, each with unique gameplay and roles:

### Tank Classes
- **Warrior** - Classic plate-wearing tank with powerful defensive abilities
- **Paladin** - Holy guardian capable of healing themselves and allies
- **Death Knight** - Resilient tank using dark powers
- **Druid** - Flexible tank shapeshifting into bear form
- **Monk** - Agile tank using martial arts
- **Demon Hunter** - Highly mobile vengeance specialization

### Healing Classes
- **Priest** - Powerful holy healer
- **Paladin** - Melee healer capable of taking damage
- **Shaman** - Restorer using elemental powers
- **Druid** - Sustained healing and resurrection abilities
- **Monk** - Healing allies through mists

### Damage Classes
- All classes have damage specializations, including Mage, Warlock, Hunter, Rogue, etc.

## Game Basics

### Level System
- Current maximum level: 70
- Levels 1-60: Azeroth Adventure
- Levels 60-70: Dragonflight content

### Item Quality
- **Gray** - Common items
- **White** - Common equipment
- **Green** - Uncommon equipment
- **Blue** - Rare equipment
- **Purple** - Epic equipment
- **Orange** - Legendary equipment

### Professions
- **Gathering Professions**: Herbalism, Mining, Skinning
- **Crafting Professions**: Alchemy, Blacksmithing, Enchanting, Engineering, Leatherworking, Tailoring, Jewelcrafting

## Start Your Adventure

### Step 1: Create Your Character
1. Choose faction and race
2. Select class
3. Customize character appearance
4. Choose starting zone

### Step 2: Learn Basic Controls
- **Movement**: WASD or arrow keys
- **Attack**: Skills on action bars
- **Interact**: Right mouse button
- **Loot**: Left mouse button

### Step 3: Complete Quests
- Yellow exclamation marks indicate available quests
- Yellow question marks indicate quest turn-ins
- Follow quest lines to progress the story

## Advanced Guide

### Dungeon System
- **Dungeons**: 5-player group content
- **Raids**: 10-30 player large-scale content
- **Heroic Difficulty**: More challenging versions
- **Mythic Difficulty**: Highest difficulty challenges

### PVP Content
- **Battlegrounds**: Team-based combat modes
- **Arenas**: 2v2, 3v3, 5v5 matches
- **World PVP**: Faction conflicts in open world

## Useful Tips

1. **Bind Your Hearthstone** - Set your home location at inns
2. **Learn Flying** - Acquire flying skills at appropriate levels
3. **Join a Guild** - Find like-minded companions
4. **Use Auction House** - Buy and sell items and materials
5. **Explore the World** - Discover hidden treasures and quests

## Getting Help

If you encounter difficulties in the game:
- Check other guides on this site
- Ask other players in the game
- Join World of Warcraft communities
- Check official forums and guides

**Remember: World of Warcraft is a vast world full of endless possibilities - enjoy your adventure!**
